Project Highlights

Connectivity

Seamless access to Upper Thomson MRT Station on the Thomson-East Coast Line. Major expressways including the Central Expressway (CTE) and Pan Island Expressway (PIE) are just minutes away, connecting you to every corner of Singapore with ease.

Lifestyle

Thomson Plaza, Ang Mo Kio Hub, and Bishan Junction 8 offer endless dining, shopping, and entertainment options. Enjoy weekend brunches, retail therapy, and family outings without traveling far from home.

Education

Within 1 km of the prestigious Ai Tong School. Nearby institutions include CHIJ St. Nicholas Girls' School, Catholic High School, and Raffles Institution, making Thomson Reserve ideal for families prioritizing quality education.

Investment

District 20 has consistently shown strong capital appreciation and rental yields. With limited new launches in this mature estate and growing demand from families and professionals, Thomson Reserve presents a compelling investment opportunity.
